Campus Marketplace - Buy & Sell Inside Your Campus

Campus Marketplace is a simple and smart platform designed especially for students to buy and sell products within their campus. Whether you want to sell something you no longer need or find great deals from others, this platform makes the process quick, easy, and hassle-free.

Sell Your Products Easily

Have something to sell? From books and notes to electronics and daily essentials, you can list any product in just a few steps. Create your account, upload product details, set your price, and make your listing visible to other students on campus.

Instant Notifications

Whenever someone shows interest in your product or wants to buy it, you’ll receive an instant notification. This helps you respond quickly and close deals faster.

Direct Communication

Our platform allows direct communication between buyers and sellers. You can discuss price, pickup location, and other details easily without any middleman.

Built for Campus Community

This platform is designed specifically for your campus community, making it easier to connect with people nearby. It builds trust and makes transactions smoother and faster.

Save Money, Reduce Waste

By buying and selling used items, you not only save money but also promote sustainability by reducing waste. It’s a smarter and more eco-friendly way to shop.

Why Choose Campus Marketplace?

  • Easy product listing
  • Fast and real-time notifications
  • Safe and trusted campus environment
  • Direct buyer-seller interaction
  • Clean and user-friendly interface
